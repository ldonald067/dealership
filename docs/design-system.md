# Design System & UX Direction

## Vibe
Fun, warm, colorful, and inviting — NOT generic SaaS or boring car-brand corporate. Think dave.com, sudowrite.com, discord.com — welcoming without being overwhelming.

## Inspiration Sites
- **dave.com** — bold green + lime accent, mascot-driven, nature tones, DM Sans body
- **sudowrite.com** — encouraging tone, customizable themes, warm and approachable
- **discord.com** — dark + vibrant accents, Poppins font, playful illustrations, animated characters

## Design Principles
1. **Bold brand color + vibrant accent** — not monochrome or muted
2. **Playful typography pairing** — personality headline font + friendly body font
3. **Illustrated/character-driven** — avoid stock photos and clinical car images
4. **Generous whitespace** — content breathes, nothing cramped
5. **Fun micro-copy** — personality in the text, not corporate-speak
6. **Subtle animations** — hover effects, state transitions, delight moments
7. **Warm dark mode** — colorful-dark like Discord, not dull gray

## Color Palette (Current)
- Primary gradient: orange-500 → rose-500
- Background: amber-50 → orange-50 → rose-50
- Accents: purple-600, emerald-500
- Shadows: orange-tinted (shadow-orange-100/50)

## Component Patterns
- Rounded corners: rounded-2xl (cards), rounded-xl (inputs/buttons)
- Cards: white bg, warm shadow, hover:shadow-lg transition
- Buttons: gradient bg, shadow-lg, rounded-xl, font-semibold
- Inputs: rounded-xl, border-gray-200, focus:border-orange-400 + ring

## Portal Differences
- **Customer portal**: luxury experience, aspirational, fun, gradient backgrounds
- **Dealer portal**: polished and functional, still has personality but more dashboard-like
